404 ARCHIVE [ongoing]
404 ARCHIVE documents the personal homepages of mid-’90s and early-’00s transgender, transsexual, and other gender-diverse communities via a network of hyperlinks recovered from the Wayback Machine.
Bergen Street Community Garden Oral History Project [ongoing]
The Bergen Street Community Garden Oral History Project records and collects the 50-year history of a community garden in Crown Heights, Brooklyn through oral history interviews and archival research.
